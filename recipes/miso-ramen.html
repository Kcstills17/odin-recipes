Recipe for Miso ramen

Miso Ramen is a delicious alternative to the usual cup ramen we all consume. Not to mention how its a more traditional Japanese dish. It does not have nearly the same amount of sodium and includes a lot more vegetables. Let's go over the steps to making it!

  • Ramen Noodles
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon of water

  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt

  • Ramen Noodle Broth
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 tablesppons of white miso
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dashi(omit for vegetarian version)

  • Ramen Noodle Toopings
  • 4 cremini of shiitake mushrooms
  • bamboo shoots(canned or fresh
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 handfuls of spinach
  • 1 handful of bean sprouts

Ramen Noodles

  1. add the flour and salt to a bowl, mix together
  2. create a bowl inside the flour and add egg & water
  3. whisk egg and slowly push in flour until all is combined
  4. knead for 2 minutes, cover in dampp cloth and rest for 30 minutes
  5. flatten dough and divide into 2 equal parts. Do this one at a time
  6. roll out dough until it is roughlt 1mm. sprinkle with flour and fold in half 2x
  7. with a sharp knife, cut into thin strips. add flour to strips and gently loosen with fingners to unfold noodles
  8. boil for 3-4 minutrs, drain and rinse with cold water

    9. Ramen Noodle Broth

  9. add vegetable stock, soy sauce, & dashi to a large soup pot. bring to a boil. reduce heat and simmer for 10 mins
  10. during the last minute, add white miso and stir to disolve
  11. add spinach and noodles to a bowl, poor in miso broth
  12. divide toppings into 2 equal parts & add to bowl
